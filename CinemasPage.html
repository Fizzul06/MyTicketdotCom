Cinemas

Classic Cinema

The Classic Cinema showcases a spacious theater with rows of comfortable red seats arranged in a tiered layout. Soft aisle lights illuminate the steps, adding warmth to the darkened auditorium. At the front, a large screen framed by gentle lighting creates a timeless movie-going atmosphere.

Kids Cinema Playground

This family-friendly cinema features colorful seating and a playful atmosphere designed for kids and families. The space includes a kids’ playground area, allowing children to play and relax before or after the movie. Bright cushions, spacious couches, and warm lighting create a fun and welcoming movie experience.

Luxury Recliner Cinema

This luxury cinema features spacious reclining seats designed for maximum comfort. Plush cushions and elegant lighting create a calm, premium movie-watching atmosphere. The modern interior offers a private and relaxing cinematic experience.
